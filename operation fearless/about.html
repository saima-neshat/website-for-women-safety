Operation Fearless !!
Safe World for Women is an not-for-profit social enterprise and multicultural movement for social justice and equality.

We provide a platform for individuals around the world to highlight issues affecting women and children.

Through our extensive social media networks, we advocate for positive social change and raise awareness about the work of our Field Partners and members of our Alliance for a Safe World for WOMEN.
Vision, Mission & Ethos

          VISION

A humane and sustainable world with compassion at its heart.
          MISSION

The Safe World International Foundation is a pioneering non-profits organisation dedicated to inspiring new worldviews through women's empowerment.

    * We bring together women from diverse cultures & backgrounds to address the root causes of poverty, hardship and  conflict.
    * By listening to women who suffer from oppression and injustice, throughout the world, we ensure that 'the voices of the silent' are heard by those who can   influence change.
    * We collaborate with others who share our vision and ethos.
    * Together, we work to promote justice and dignity for women and children.
    * Independent from government, corporate or national interests, we highlight abuse of human rights throughout the world,   educate and mobilize the   public, and campaign to improve policy and attitudes.
    * We do all this to enrich the lives of women and children, socially and economically, and bring about a safer, fairer world for all.

                                                                      A Safe World for Women is a safe world for all
          ETHOS

         + To maintain the utmost respect for all human life and for the natural environment.
                + To practice honesty, integrity and trustworthiness.
                + To communicate sensitively and compassionately.
                + To promote cooperation in preference to competition.
                + To carry out business and enterprise in a sustainable and socially responsible way.
                + Not to permit considerations of age, disease or disability, creed, ethnic origin, gender, nationality, race, sexual  orientation, social standing or any other factor to affect attitude towards any person
